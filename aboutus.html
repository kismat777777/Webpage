About Education Guru NP

Education Guru NP is a leading online platform providing high-quality educational content for students from grades 6 to 12. We make learning accessible, interactive, and engaging in both English and Nepali mediums.

Our platform offers video lessons, tutorials, and learning resources to help students succeed academically.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, develop critical thinking skills, and foster a love for lifelong learning through innovative educational content.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted online educational platform in Nepal, inspiring students to reach their full potential.
